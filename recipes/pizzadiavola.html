Pizza Diavola

Description

Pizza Diavola is a spicy Italian pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, spicy salami (such as soppressata or pepperoni), and chili peppers. The dough is made by mixing water, salt, and yeast with flour, and it's known for its flavorful and fiery taste.

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella (Fior di latte)
  • Spicy Salami (Soppressata or Pepperoni)
  • Chili Peppers or Red Pepper Flakes
